My Favorite Recipe:

Blue Berry/ Raspberry Muffin Mix

Ingredients:

  • 2 Brown eggs
  • 1/2 box of Jiffy Blue Berry Cornbread
  • 1/2 Box of Jiffy Raspberry Cornbread Mix
  • 1 Box of Jiffy CornBread Mix
  • 1/2 Cup of Whole Milk
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract

Instructions:

  1. Place all items into mixing bowl except the stick of butter
  2. Proceed to mix ingredients
  3. Butter the baking pan
  4. Empty the batter into the baking pan
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until Golden Brown
  6. Spread butter across the pan
  7. Let sit for 20 minutes
  8. Eat & ENJOY!

This is my favorite recipe because my grandmother likes it, so I love it. She often reminds me of how in her day cornbread was a meal in itself. However...Blue/Raspberry Cornbread was every poor black child's favorite treat. The simple things in life.
