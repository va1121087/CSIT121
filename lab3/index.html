MY Favorite Recipe:

Blue Berry/ Raspberry Cornbread

  • 2 Brown eggs
  • 1/2 box of Jiffy Blue Berry Cornbread
  • 1/2 Box of Jiffy Raspberry Cornbread Mix
  • 1 Box of Jiffy Cornbread Mix
  • 1/2 Cup of Whole Milk
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Vanilla Extract

PREHEAT OVEN TO 400*

  1. Place all items into mixing bowl except the stick of butter

  2. Proceed to mix ingredients until it develops a lump-free consistency
  3. Butter the baking pan generously on all sides to ensure Cornbread doesn't burn/stick to pan
  4. Proceed to empty the batter into the baking pan, then gently insert into oven
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until Golden Brown
  6. Remove from oven, and IMMEDIATELY spread what's left of the entire stick of butter across the entire pan of cornbread
  7. Let sit for 20 minutes
  8. Eat & ENJOY!!!!!!

This is my favorite recipe because it has become a staple of my community, feeding many impoverished black families for generations. My grandmother will be 98 years old in 90 days. She often reminds me of how much things were different in her day when black people in America were not afforded educational/economic opportunity because of racism and poverty and institutional racism. She tells me how little things like food were a big deal, and cornbread was a meal by itself, and Blue/Raspberry Cornbread with butter was every poor black child's favorite treat.
